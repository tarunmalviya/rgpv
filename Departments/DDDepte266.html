University Dual Degree Integrated PG Program
View Profile View Learning Resources
This university is established in the year 1998, by Madhya Pradesh Vidhan Sabha Act 13, 1998. Over a sprawling Campus of about 247 acres, the RGPV is marching towards development into a center of excellence in the arena of Technical Education, Research and Innovations. University offers PhD and 69 PG and UG courses. University Dual Degree Integrated PG Programme is an initiative of Rajiv Gandhi Prodyogiki Vishwavidyalay to provide quality education to students with extraordinary skills. Started in the year 2011, this programme is offered in the six disciplines of engineering with the intake of 20 each. Duration of this course is Five year. The students of DDI-PG prgramme will get either BE and MTech degree or BE and MBA degree on the successful completion of the course. This programme is offered in the autonomous UTDs of the university.

Courses Offered

 DDI-PG-Civil Engineering
  DDI-PG-Computer Science & Engineering
 DDI-PG-Electrical Engineering
  DDI-PG-Electronics & Communication Engineering
 DDI-PG-Information Technology
  DDI-PG-Mechanical Engineering
